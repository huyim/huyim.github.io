Project

  1. Human-Drone Collaborations in Firefighting

    智能无人机在消防领域的应用

    IMRG UZH & Fire Department in Silenen and Altdorf · 2022.07 — 2023.03 大学与消防局合作项目 · 2022.07 — 2023.03

    This was a group project involving 5 team members focused on studying the interaction of autonomous multi-drones in firefighting scenarios and public opinions. The project consisted of four major phases: scenario design, development, two experiments, and evaluations.

    - Designed surveys and interview questions for data collection.
    - Analyzed 8 preliminary interviews with firefighters to design scenarios and create user stories, as well as 12 interviews with firefighters and other experiment participants for the final evaluation.
    - Led the technology aspect of the project, utilizing Vue.js and Python Flask to develop a commander interface for interacting with the drones.
    - The iterative revisions made to the interface between the two experiments resulted in an improved rating, increasing from 3.0/5.0 to 4.5/5.0.
    - Strengthened collaboration and communication skills within the group.

    项目背景：探究无人机在山区消防救援的场景应用并参加两场模拟演习

    - 实地走访3个本地消防局，参与无人机实时互动、无人机搜索等场景设计与文档撰写
    - 负责人机平台“实时画面”和“消息框”的功能设计以及平台总体推进
    - 通过反思总结第一次演习失利，与团队成员优化改进职责划分、团队内部沟通以及团队与消防员协调沟通等方面，最终第二次演习成功并获得消防指挥员认可
